Do you have something to shop from Costco, Walmart, BJ's or other shopping locations and brought / delivered to Ghana? All you need to do is place your order with us and it will get shipped to the destination safely without damage.

After receiving your order, we shop according to your requirements, load the pallet in the East Hartford Warehouse and transport the pallet to New York Harbor. The ship sails from Africa-Ghana to the Capital Accra port. The pallet passes the customs and then goes straight to the Akusan Ventures LLC Warehouse in Accra. Akusan Ventures LLC then delivers your shipment to the destination- right at the doorstep.

With Akusan, the world is closer and you don’t have to wait to purchase things across the boundaries. We are always available to lend a hand to get what you require and deliver it to you, in the shortest time possible.
